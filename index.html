About Us

Your love for good & tasty native andhra food in Dublin is the reason why we’ve
been bringing back the native recipies. Welcome to Peacock restaurant

Traditional Andhra Cusine

Peacock is a fine dine & take away restaurant located in Dublin, serving authentic andhra food, At Peacock We follow the traditional form of cooking in all our food preparation, we believe in using the most natural ingredients and spices in our food.
If you are looking for the authentic fish pulusu of east godavary or special stuffed crabs then Peacock is the place to visit. our kodi pulao is rated best in Dublin. At Peacock, we always believe in reviving the most authentic and traditional recipies that are lost in time.
